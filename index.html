Who we are

IgniteHer Media Network is a refugee, youth, and women-led Community-Based Organization (CBO) based in Kiryandongo Refugee Settlement.

We believe in the power of storytelling and digital media to amplify voices, challenge societal norms, and create positive change. Through our programs, we empower marginalized communities by providing a platform for advocacy, education, and creative expression.

Vision

To envision a world where empowered communities stand united in honoring, protecting and championing the rights of women, girls and the future fathers.

Mission

To advocate for the rights of girls, women , and future fathers through media-driven initiatives while fostering peaceful coexistence, cultural preservation, and talent development among refugee and host communities.

Our Commitment

IgniteHer Media Network is committed to empowering girls, women and youths, amplifying marginalized voices, and fostering a culture of peace and coexistence. Through our work, we strive to bridge gaps, inspire action, and create a more inclusive society where every voice is heard and valued

Leadership Structure

Our leadership is composed of strong, dedicated women from diverse backgrounds who bring unique perspectives and expertise to our work:

  • Chairperson : A Sudanese national providing strategic leadership and vision.

  • Finance Officer : A national ensuring financial accountability and transparency.

  • Secretary : A registered Kenyan refugee responsible for administration, coordination, and communication.

Membership Structure

IgniteHer Media Network is a dedicated group of individuals working together to create positive change in their communities. Our team consists of diverse members who bring unique perspectives and experiences to our mission.

Our diverse team ensures that our advocacy for girls', women's and youths rights, peaceful coexistence, cultural promotion, and talent development is enriched by various lived experiences and perspectives. Together, we are committed to transforming communities through media-driven initiatives.

Legal Status& Location

IgniteHer Media Network is a legally registered CBO with its office located in Cluster N of Kiryandongo Refugee Settlement.

We also hold an operational permit from the district authorities, granting us the authorization to conduct activities within the settlement and beyond.
